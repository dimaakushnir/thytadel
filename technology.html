Our technology operates at the intersection of computer vision, risk modelling, and operational decision systems. It was born in extreme and competitive water environments, but designed from the outset to scale across assets, venues, and public spaces.

View patent

Thytadel builds AI systems that observe, understand, and coordinate response across real environments - where human attention is limited and the cost of failure is high.

Description

Safety is an information problem

In complex environments, incidents are rarely caused by a single failure. They emerge from incomplete visibility, delayed recognition, and fragmented decision-making. Thytadel addresses this by creating a shared operational picture - transforming live data into clear, prioritised signals that operators can act on immediately. Our systems are designed to reduce uncertainty, not generate noise.

How it works

The Thytadel platform continuously processes live inputs from physical environments and converts them into operational intelligence.

(01)
Sense

The platform ingests real-time inputs from video feeds, sensors, and operational systems across water, venues, assets, and facilities. It is designed to integrate with existing infrastructure wherever possible.

(02)
Understand

AI models analyse behaviour, motion, and environmental context to detect incidents, anomalies, and unsafe conditions. Events are evaluated against risk models that prioritise severity and urgency.

(03)
Decide

The system ranks events and determines what requires action now - and what does not. This ensures operators focus on the right problem at the right moment

(04)
Act

Alerts, workflows, and notifications are triggered according to predefined operational logic. Human teams remain in control, supported by clear, time-critical information.

Beyond

Beyond single-use safety systems

Thytadel’s earliest deployments focused on aquatic incidents - environments defined by low visibility, high noise, and minimal reaction time. These constraints shaped the platform’s design and resulted in UK Patent GB2623311. The same operating conditions exist far beyond water. Today, the platform extends into broader risk domains - identifying unsafe situations, anomalous behaviour, and operational threats across diverse environments.

Applied

Designed for complex environments

The platform is built to operate where conventional monitoring systems fail. The system is environment-agnostic by design. Deployment logic adapts to risk profiles, not industries.

Water parks, marinas, and aquatic facilities

Sailing events and offshore operations

Public venues and recreational infrastructure

Asset-heavy environments (yachts, vehicles, properties)

Mixed-use and operational facilities

Deploy intelligence where it matters

Thytadel works with public bodies, operators, and organisations that require safety systems capable of operating in the real world - not controlled environments. We engage selectively, prioritising deployments where the platform can deliver measurable impact.
