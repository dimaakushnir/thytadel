Company Roots

Thytadel's origins stem from a competitive sailing career spanning over three decades. Alexander's father competed in offshore races, where precision, discipline, and safety were paramount. Concurrently, he pursued a career in civil engineering, designing resilient systems for unpredictable conditions. Immersed in marinas, workshops, regattas, and engineering blueprints, Alexander inherited dual insights: practical maritime expertise and a systematic engineering approach.
